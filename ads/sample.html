Trstdly - Finally, the details of the accident that happened to actor Jeremy Renner were revealed to the public. The Washoe County Sheriff's Office, Nevada, United States of America, held a press conference on Tuesday, January 3, 2023, local time.

Information regarding the incident when the Marvel actor was cleaning snow was revealed.

The results of the investigation by the authorities, this incident started when Jeremy Renner's vehicle got stuck in the snow.

But driving this car is not Jeremy Renner but his relative.

For your information, when converted, the weight of this 14,330-pound vehicle is almost 6,500 kilograms or more than 6 tons.

After successfully towing the trapped car, Jeremy Renner got out of his PistenBully.

But unexpectedly, the PistenBully suddenly started driving without a driver.

Witnesses saw Jeremy Renner trying to get back into the driver's seat. But he was not seen again until the PistenBully stopped because of a pile of snow on the road in front of his house.

The authorities confirmed that there was no suspicion of criminal activity in this incident.

"We believe this was a tragic accident," they explained.
